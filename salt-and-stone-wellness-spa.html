1.1

Client's Brief

Brand

Salt & Stone is a premium wellness spa known for its grounding rituals, warm-toned interiors, and sensory-focused treatments. The brand carries a sense of stillness and calm that sets it apart from faster-paced competitors.

Purpose

To create content that mirrors the actual experience of being inside Salt & Stone. The brand's philosophy centers on slow, sensory wellness, and every post, story, and visual needed to carry that same quiet energy across social media.

Background

Salt & Stone had a clear identity in person. The interiors, the rituals, the pace of the treatments all communicated something specific. But the digital presence didn't carry that weight. The brand needed cohesive content that communicated its offerings with the same depth and calm that clients felt when they walked through the door.

Creative Direction

Refined, slow-paced, and grounded in warmth. Visuals are styled with natural textures, muted earthy tones, and spacious compositions. Photography focuses on hands, materials, and the sensory details of the spa experience. The copy is minimal and measured. Nothing is loud. Everything breathes.
