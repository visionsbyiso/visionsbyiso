Case Study

Sunday Skin Center

Social media management, content creation, and brand identity for a modern skin clinic.

Challenge, direction, outcome

Challenge

Digital touchpoints felt fragmented.

The in-clinic experience was premium, but social communication lacked consistency and clear visual hierarchy.

Direction

Clinical clarity with editorial warmth.

We used restrained layouts, calm color pacing, and image-led storytelling to build trust without visual noise.

Outcome

A more coherent, high-trust presence.

Content became immediately recognizable and easier to scale across launches, treatments, and evergreen education.

Services + timeline

Long-term social system support.

Services: social management, content creation, brand identity. Timeline: multi-year engagement with seasonal refreshes.
